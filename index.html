Texas Bugs!

Big Bug Legends

Watch out for the big bugs!

I was 7 years old the first time I moved to Texas. We were stationed in England and one of our neighbors had just moved there from Texas. The little girl who was my age warned me that there were GIANT bugs in Texas and to be careful. I was terrified.


Luckily, it wasn't as bad as she made it seem, like this pretty guy here.


A Butterfly! Or as my Grandma would call it, a Flutterby!

Okay, there are some big creepy crawlies though!

I think all the orbweavers of Texas are gorgeous and I respect and appreciate them eating flies and mosquitos. However, I do not want them on me and I hate running into their webs.


Orb Weaver!

You can't deny they are beautiful

I think these giant grasshoppers are so pretty and incredible, some are as long as my hand!
